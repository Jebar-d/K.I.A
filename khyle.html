This project was one of my exercises to practice working with tables, text formatting, and basic webpage structure.

The calendar displays the month of January 2025 and includes important dates like:

January 1 – New Year's Day
January 8 – My Birthday 😊
January 28 – Lunar New Year

I used a table to organize the days of the week, styled the headers with a red background and white text, and used different font colors to highlight the special events.

The layout is basic, but it helped me understand how to use <table>, <tr>, <td>, and how to apply simple inline CSS to customize colors and alignment.
